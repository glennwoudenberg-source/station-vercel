The Station

A wedding is a happy and busy event for both the wedding party and the guests. Convenience and accessibility go a long way towards making that special day less stressful. The Station is your exclusive option to stay close to your Nunica wedding venue while enjoying a large, comfortable private residence.

Living Space

A spacious, open-concept living area centered around a 100-inch high-definition television and premium audio setup. Black steel accents, natural wood finishes, and oversized seating create a bold yet comfortable environment.

Fireplace Lounge

A secondary seating area anchored by a modern fireplace and warm wood tones. Perfect for relaxing after the celebration or enjoying a quiet moment.

Kitchen

The kitchen is stylish and open to the living space, offering a clean modern layout with everything needed for your stay. A nearby half bath is easily accessible for guests.

Bedroom & Bath

A private bedroom retreat with an attached en suite bath designed for comfort and indulgence. The space features an oversized shower, fresh towels, a towel warmer, and convenient access to a full-size washer and dryer.

Your Stay

This is more than a hotel room — it is a complete residence designed for comfort, privacy, and convenience. Perfect for a couple who want to stay close, unwind in comfort, and enjoy a private space before and after the celebration.
